About Our School

Established in 1965, Kibingo Catholic School has been a beacon of excellence in education for over five decades. We are committed to providing holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and character development.

Our mission is to develop well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the modern world while maintaining strong Catholic values and principles.
